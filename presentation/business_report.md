# Business Insights Report

## Objective

This analytics suite enables decision-makers to predict customer churn risk, forecast revenue trends, and estimate property values using machine learning.

---

## Customer Churn Insights

Key churn drivers identified:

contract type  
monthly charges  
internet service type  

Customers on month-to-month contracts show higher churn probability.

Business action:

Offer long-term contract incentives.

---

## Sales Forecasting Insights

Revenue predictions influenced by:

quantity sold  
seasonality (month)  
regional variation  

Business action:

Adjust inventory allocation by region and seasonal demand.

---

## House Price Insights

Top price drivers:

area  
location  
property type  

Urban properties consistently show higher valuation.

Business action:

Use predictions for pricing strategy and investment prioritization.

---

## Strategic Value

This solution enables:

data-driven pricing decisions  
customer retention strategy optimization  
regional revenue planning  

Machine learning improves forecasting reliability compared to manual estimation methods.
